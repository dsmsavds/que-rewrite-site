Our Features

Antinuke

If a non-whitelisted user repeatedly creates, edits, or deletes channels or roles, webhooks, or emojis, or performs actions such as banning, muting, or kicking members multiple times within a short period, Que will automatically ban the user and restore any affected changes.

New

Antibot

After activation, Que will verify whether the bot you have invited to your server is a Verified Bot. If the bot is not verified, Que will automatically kick the unverified bot from the server.


New

Antilink

Upon activation, Que will monitor all channels for any links posted by users. If a user sends a link, Que will remove the message and issue a warning to the user.
